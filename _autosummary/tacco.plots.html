Visualization functions

Functions

annotated_heatmap

Plots a heatmap of cells and genes grouped by categorical annotations.

annotation_coordinate

Plots an annotation density with respect to a scalar coordinate.

cellsize

Scatter plots of average cell sizes in an annotation category in the whole dataset against some reference dataset.

co_occurrence

Plot co-occurrence as determined by co_occurrence().

co_occurrence_matrix

Plot co-occurrence as determined by co_occurrence() or co_occurrence_matrix() as a matrix.

comparison

Scatterplots of the annotation fractions of different annotations, e.g. of different annotation methods or of methods and a ground truth.

compositions

Plot compositions of groups.

contribution

Plot contribution to groups.

dotplot

Dot plot of expression values.

frequency

Scatter plots of the total frequency of annotation in the whole dataset against some reference dataset.

frequency_bar

Bar plots of the total frequency of annotation in the whole dataset against some reference dataset.

get_default_colors

Chooses default colors.

heatmap

Plot heatmap of contribution to groups.

mix_base_colors

Mix colors "additively".

scatter

Scatter plots of annotation.

sigmap

Plot heatmap of contribution to groups and mark significant differences with asterisks.

significances

Plot enrichment significances.

subplots

Creates a new figure with a grid of subplots.
